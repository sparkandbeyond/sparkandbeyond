About Us

OUR STORY

Spark & Beyond was built from the ground up — by the community, for the community.

"Uniting communities and providing opportunities close to home."
Our Mission
Who We Are

BUILT FROM THE COMMUNITY UP

Spark & Beyond is a youth sports organization rooted in master-planned communities across the Cedar Park, Leander, and Round Rock area.

We go to the neighborhood. We partner with HOAs, hire local coaches, and build programs around what each community actually needs — not just sports, but confidence, communication, and skills that last a lifetime.

How It Started

FROM ONE COURT TO A MOVEMENT

01
The Problem

A neighborhood full of kids. No local programs.

Travisso, TX had everything — except somewhere for kids to play and grow together. Parents were driving across town. Families barely knew each other. That didn't sit right.

02
The Solution

Bring the program to the community.

We didn't build a facility — we partnered with the HOA, found local coaches, and launched Travisso Sports League right on the community court. No commute. No friction. Just show up.

03
What's Next

A model that works anywhere.

TSL proved it. Now Spark & Beyond is the engine behind taking that same approach to new communities across Cedar Park, Leander, and Round Rock — and beyond.

⚡ Founded 2025

Founded by Neer

Spark & Beyond was built on a simple observation: the best communities are the ones where families actually know each other. Sports are the fastest way to build that. So we built the programs to make it happen — right in your neighborhood, not across town.
